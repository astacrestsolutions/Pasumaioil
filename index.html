Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Care

Pasumai was born in the heart of a small village in Tamil Nadu, where the art of cold-pressed oil making has been a cherished tradition for centuries. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the purest, most authentic oils to your kitchen.

Using ancient chekku methods and carefully selected seeds, we extract oils that retain all their natural nutrients, flavor, and aroma. No chemicals, no heat processing – just pure, honest oil the way nature intended.
